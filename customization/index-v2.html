Common questions about custom projects

Real answers, no fluff.

How long does a typical custom project take?

Most projects scope to 2–6 weeks once we have your inputs. Smaller fixes can ship in 3–7 days. Larger features (custom dashboards, integrations) can run 4–8 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you offer fixed quotes or hourly?

Fixed quotes for scoped projects — you know the price before we start. Hourly only for ongoing dedicated developer engagements (separate offering).

What does your 1-year warranty cover?

Anything we built breaks because of our code — we fix it free, no questions. Excludes changes to your underlying Brilliant Directories software version or other developer changes.

Can you work with our existing developer?

Yes, often. We coordinate scope so we don't overlap. Your developer handles their lane; we handle ours. Status updates kept transparent across both.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, standard mutual NDA available on request before discovery. We've signed hundreds across founders, agencies, and enterprise clients.

What if the project scope changes mid-build?

We send a Change Request with a revised quote and timeline. You approve or scope back to original. No silent scope creep, no surprise invoices.

How do payments work?

50% to start, 50% on delivery for most projects. Larger projects can be milestone-based (33/33/33). Payment via wire, Razorpay, or Stripe.

Can you also help with strategy — not just execution?

For project work, our team ships what you spec. If you want strategic input on the business itself, look at our Founder's Track — apply-only partnership with Yakin Shah.

Ready to scope your custom project?

Two ways to start. Both go to the same team. Pick whichever's easier for you.
