## Executive note

One **hub** (`/grow-with-ai`) frames “why AI + BD now.” **Spokes** match search and stage: dev capacity, fleet packaging, site-specific AI, automation, SEO. Messaging overlaps on purpose (repeated proof, same buyer); differentiation is **intent**, **objection**, and **next step**. Converge on four commercial paths: **AI Dev Fleet**, **Dedicated Developer**, **Growth Plan** tiers, **bundles**. Catalog language already reads **implemented outcomes** (fixed price, delivery, warranty)—reposition “tools” as **solutions / add-ons** in copy without renaming URLs overnight. Sources: [llms.txt](https://bdgrowthsuite.com/llms.txt), [tools listing](https://bdgrowthsuite.com/tools).

## Nav / Services clarification

**Zoom Clinics** does not belong under **Services** in the mental model you want: it is a **Setup / Launch** accelerator (time-boxed, cohort, “get unstuck and ship”), or a **top-level promo** (hero, banner, “New: Zoom Clinics”), not a long-tail SKU next to Maintenance or Customization. Prior sitemap error: treating Clinics like a **service line** in the same dropdown. Cleaner IA: **Services** = ongoing or scoped professional lines (setup, maintenance, customization, consultation, hire developer, growth plans); **Zoom Clinics** = **path to first value** or **campaign**, linked from home, setup, or a “Start here” strip—not buried as a sixth Services child.

## Solutions categories (from live content)

**Argument:** [llms.txt](https://bdgrowthsuite.com/llms.txt) describes the catalog as tools with **“implementation details, delivery times, warranties, and licensing.”** The [tools](https://bdgrowthsuite.com/tools) page shows **Fixed Price / Starts From / SUBSCRIPTION**, outcome-led blurbs (“complete … solution—not just displaying hours”), and categories (Onboarding, UI UX, Integration, SEO, Enhance Conversions, CRM, etc.). That is **purchased implementation**, not self-serve SaaS where the user clicks and configures alone.

| High-level category | Evidence (names / quotes) | Hub page? | SKU page? |
|---------------------|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| **Structured data & AI-visible content** | “Blog Schema…”, “Job Schema…”, “Turn every blog post into a clear, AI-understandable knowledge source” | **Yes — hub** (“Schema & AI-ready content”) | Each schema SKU |
| **Member profile & directory UX** | Business Highlights, Photo Gallery, Introduction Video, tab edits, custom profile detail | **Yes — hub** (“Member experience”) | Individual SKUs |
| **Conversion & lead capture** | Lead Gen on search / no results, custom signup/checkout, landing page design | **Yes — hub** (“Conversion & growth”) | Per SKU |
| **Integrations & automation** | Constant Contact signup automation; Integration category | **Yes — hub** | Per integration |
| **SEO & URL / content ops** | Edit Post URLs (“Better SEO & Branding”), FAQ plugin (“boost SEO”), table of contents | **Partial hub** or merge with schema hub if thin | SKU each |
| **Trust & social proof surfaces** | Latest Reviews on search results | Often **module** under Conversion hub | Optional SKU page |
| **Branding / copy / consultation** | Categories on listing | **Link to services** more than tool hub | Mix |

**SKU pages** for high volume or high revenue one-offs; **category hubs** where many SKUs share one buyer question (“improve member profiles,” “fix SEO/structured data”).

## Growth Plan — rename candidates

Keep URL **`/growth-plans`**; change **display** name only if desired.

| Candidate | Pros | Cons |
|-----------|------|------|
| **Growth Advisory** | Clear DFY + strategy; matches “founder-level guidance” in [llms.txt](https://bdgrowthsuite.com/llms.txt) | Less “productized”; sounds expensive |
| **BD Growth Membership** | Recurring value, community feel; fits Lite/Pro/Elite | “Membership” can imply DIY community—not your core |
| **Launch & Scale Partner** | Stage narrative (launch → scale) | Long; less obvious vs “Growth Plans” |
| **Revenue Accelerator** | Punchy, outcome tone | Can sound hypey; may blur with marketing agencies |
| **Operator’s Growth Desk** | “Apply-only” / elite vibe without saying it | Jargon; needs one-line explanation |

**Tone map:** *Apply-only* → Operator’s Growth Desk, Growth Advisory. *Membership* → BD Growth Membership. *Accelerator* → Revenue Accelerator (use carefully).

## SEO growth inside Growth Plan?

**Recommendation:** Keep **SEO/growth** as its **own spoke** (`/seo-growth` or similar) **and** a **named section** inside Growth Plan sales narrative—not a separate product tab that duplicates the whole spoke.

| Option | SEO | Clarity |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Separate spoke only** | Best for long-tail queries, backlinks, dedicated meta | Risk: disconnect from “Growth” bundle story |
| **Nested tab only** | Weak URL depth for SEO; thin tab can feel hidden | Good if SEO is truly subordinate |
| **Canonical merge** | One strong URL; no cannibalization | Loses specific SEO-intent traffic and testing |
| **Spoke + in-plan section (recommended)** | Spoke ranks for “BD SEO / schema / growth”; internal links pass relevance to `/growth-plans` | Buyer sees SEO as **included lever** in Growth, **detail** on spoke |

Use **`rel=canonical`** only if two URLs are truly duplicate; otherwise **cross-link** spoke ↔ Growth Plan with distinct H1s and intents.
